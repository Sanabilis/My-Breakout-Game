SPACE = Start the game
P = Pause the game
R = Restart the game
    - Restarting after victory keeps the score and lives
    - Otherwise, the whole game is reset

RIGHT, LEFT = Move the paddles

1 = Perspective camera
2 = Top-down camera
3 = Free camera
    - LEFT CLICK = Rotate camera
    - RIGHT CLICK = Move camera
    - MOUSE WHEEL = Zoom
    - This was mostly meant for testing and can be used to move outside of boundaries. It is not really meant for gameplay.
